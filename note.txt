ğŸ¯ Explain BFS in 2 lines:

BFS explores neighbors level by level and always finds the shortest path in an unweighted grid.

ğŸ¯ Explain A* Search:

A* uses
f(n) = g(n) + h(n)
to find optimal paths faster than BFS.

ğŸ¯ What is a Heuristic?

A function that estimates the cost to reach the goal (ex: Manhattan distance).

ğŸ¯ Why use deep copy in Treasure Grab?

To prevent scoring errors by freezing the grid state before agents move.

ğŸ¯ Why use greedy logic in Snake & Prey?

Greedy decisions work well in fast-moving competitive environments.

ğŸ¯ Why conflict cells marked X in Maze Domination?

To ensure fairness when both agents try to claim the same territory.
